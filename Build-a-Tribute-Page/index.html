Sam Harris

Author, Neuroscientist, and Philosopher.

Here's a timeline of Sam Harris' Life:

  • 1967 - born in Los Angeles, California
  • 1986 - Went to India, where he studied meditation with Buddhist and Hindu religious teachers
  • 2000 - Received B.A. in Philosophy from Stanford University
  • 2004 - Wrote his first book called The End of Faith: Religion, Terror, and the Future of Reason
  • 2005 - Appeared in the documentary film The God Who Wasn't There.
  • 2006 - Wrote his second book called Letter to a Christian Nation
  • 2007 - Founded Project Reason, a charitable foundation devoted to spreading scientific knowledge
  • 2009 - Received Ph.D. in Cognitive Neuroscience from University of California
  • 2010 - Wrote his third book called The Moral Landscape: How Science Can Determine Human Values
  • 2015 - Announced that he could no longer ethically justify eating meat and had returned to vegetarianism
